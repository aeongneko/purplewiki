## How to use spam/pyramid commands?
<Image src="/images/faq/spam.png" width={128} height={0} />
### spam
`/spam (count) (delay) (text) (text_optional)`\
usage: `/spam 5 3 hi`\
tip: use [zwsp](https://zerowidthspace.me) in `text_optional` is better to spam :P

### pyramid
`/spam pyramid (width) (emote) (delay)`\
usage: `/spam pyramid 5 DinoDance 3`
